FAQ

What is CatXapp?

CatXapp is an iPhone app for scrap yards and catalytic converter buyers. Search converter codes, see live PGM-adjusted buy prices, build carts, set margin, and export PDF receipts.

How do converter prices update?

CatXapp adjusts catalog prices using live platinum, palladium, and rhodium spot markets. Tap refresh on the market strip to pull the latest spots.

Essential vs Pro?

Essential covers unlimited code lookup and live PGM prices. Pro adds cart tools, profit margin control, saved loads, and PDF export.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — Pro monthly includes a 14-day free trial through the App Store. Cancel anytime in Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions.

Is CatXapp on Android?

No. CatXapp is available on iPhone only (iOS).

Are prices official ACC prices?

Prices are estimates based on catalog data and live metal markets. Always verify before buying.
