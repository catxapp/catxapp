Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

catXapp (“we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Waitlist signups: If you join our pre-launch waitlist, we collect the name and email you submit through our Google Form.
  • Support requests: If you contact us through our support form, we collect the name, email, optional phone number, and message you submit.
  • App usage: catXapp stores preferences locally on your device (cart items, saved carts, margin settings). We do not operate a user account server.
  • Subscriptions: Payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

How we use information

  • To notify waitlist subscribers when catXapp launches on the App Store.
  • To respond to support requests you send through our website.
  • To provide app functionality on your device (search, pricing, cart).
  • To fetch live precious metal spot prices from third-party sources for price calculations.

Third-party services

catXapp may request data from Kitco and similar public market data sources to calculate live prices. Waitlist and support forms are hosted by Google Forms. Subscriptions are managed by Apple App Store.

Our marketing site may use the Meta Pixel (Facebook) to measure ad performance when you visit from a paid campaign. The Pixel collects usage data such as page views and waitlist signups according to Meta’s privacy policy. You can control ad tracking in your device and browser settings.

Data retention

Waitlist and support data are retained until you request removal or until no longer needed. App data on your device can be removed by deleting the app.

Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of your waitlist or support information by contacting us. For App Store subscription management, use your Apple ID account settings.

Children

catXapp is intended for business users in the scrap and recycling industry. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the app? Use our support form.

Disclaimer

catXapp provides price estimates based on catalog data and live metal markets. Prices are not guaranteed. Always verify before purchasing catalytic converters.
